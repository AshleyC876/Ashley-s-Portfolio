About Me

Hello! I'm Ashley Chambers, an international student from Jamaica
pursuing my passion for technology at Voorhees University—an HBCU
(Historically Black Colleges and Universities). Currently on track to
graduate in May 2026, I am majoring in Computer Science with a keen focus
on becoming a proficient software developer, specializing in front-end
development and UX/UI design.

My journey in the world of technology is driven by a deep-seated passion
for bringing designs to life through code. I believe that the intersection
of technology and creativity is where innovation thrives. I aspire to create
seamless and visually captivating user experiences that not only meet but
exceed expectations.

Join me on this exciting journey as I explore the boundless opportunities
that technology has to offer. Together, let's create a future where technology
is not just functional but also a work of art. Thank you for visiting my corner
of the web—where ideas come to life through code!
